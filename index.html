1) Getting Started

How do I create an account?

Download CleanSlate from the App Store and sign up with your phone number.

What devices are supported?

iPhone running the iOS version listed on our App Store page. We aim to support the latest two iOS releases.

2) Linking & Unlinking Accounts (Plaid)

Linking?

Use Link Credit Card on the Cards tab or in Manage Connections.

How do I unlink an account?

In the app, open the linked account and choose Unlink. You can also revoke access from your bank’s settings (look for “Connected Apps”).

What data does CleanSlate access?

Read‑only data such as account name/type, masked identifiers, balances, and transactions (amount, date, merchant, category) to display insights.

3) Privacy & Data

Do you store my bank login or full card numbers?

No. We do not receive or store your banking credentials. Connections are handled by Plaid.

Can I request a copy or deletion of my data?

Yes. Email us at privacy@cleanslate.app with your request from the email associated with your account.

See our Privacy Policy for full details.

4) Troubleshooting

Sync issues

  • Pull to refresh on the Accounts screen.
  • Confirm your bank isn’t blocking third‑party access.
  • Unlink and re‑link the account if needed.

App crashes or freezes

  • Update to the latest iOS and app version.
  • Force close and reopen the app.
  • Reboot your device.

Wrong or missing transactions

  • Wait a few minutes—banks sometimes delay updates.
  • Try refresh; if still missing, unlink/re‑link.
  • Contact us with the merchant name, amount, and date.
